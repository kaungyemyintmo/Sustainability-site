Why is it important to save the earth?

We all know earth is our one and only home. In the solar system, there is no other planet as habitable for us, humans, and animals, as the Earth. Due to inconsiderate actions done in the past by irresponsible people, the earth has to suffer. Throughout generations, our beautiful earth has gone through drastic changes for the worse. Therefore, it is our duty to help save the earth while we still can. There are so many ways to save the environment and restore our earth's health and beauty. I, myself, have set up three personal goals to help heal our earth.

Refuse, Reuse, Recycle

Currently, land pollution is one of the biggest problems we are facing. It is our duty as patriotic citizens to lessen our production of waste and always follow these three steps, which are to refuse it when we are offered plastc items, to reuse and recycle them whenever possible.

Conserve Water

There are so many countries in the world currently facing the problem of shortage of clean water. Therefore, we should be grateful for the water that we use daily and try to conserve it by closing taps and sinks while shaving or brushing teeth, and taking showers instead of baths.

Drive Less

Carbon emission is the primary contributor to air pollution. Out of all kinds of carbon emission, the emission of exhaust fumes from cars and vehicles pollute our air the most. Therefore, we should drive less, take carpools, use public transport, and bike to places.
