Climate Change

The adverse effects that the earth has been suffering from include climate change, the gradual disruption of the earth's ozone layers, increase in temperatures and more. It is our duty as patriotic citizens of the earth to prevent this in every way we can. Due to rapid climate change, many different species of flora and fauna have gone extinct or on the verge of extinction. Here is a list of endangered and extinct animals due to climate change.
