About the Author

I'm a year1 IT student at SP. I moved from Myanmar to Singapore to pursue my further studies. More than IT, I'm passionate about making a difference in the world. I believe that technology can be a powerful tool for positive change, and I'm excited to be part of that movement. I am also a strong advocate for sustainability and environmental conservation.

Interests

Outside of my tech endeavours, I have a knack for handicrafts. I started cross-stitching when I was 10. From cross-stitch, the passion slowly grew in me and I used to operate my own online crocheted stuffed toy store!

Personality

I am usually very outgoing since I love being in social surroundings. Exploring different places- both local and overseas, is my hobby. I am also very open-minded and I love to try new things. The MBTI personality test says I am an ESFP or the Entertainer.

Experience

I have a passion for volunteering and helping out the community. I have volunteered at various events and activities, such as One Heart by Republic Polytechnic. I also have experience in event planning and management, having organized several events for my school and community.
